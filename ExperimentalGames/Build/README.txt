CONTROLS
========

Move Rope Left:	 	A
Move Rope Right: 	D
Move Rope Forward:	W
Move Rope Back: 	S
Move Rope Up:		Left Shift
Move Rope Down:		Left Ctrl

Drop Block: 		Space

Move Camera Left:	Z
Move Camera Right:	X


To solve level: -pick up red button and place it just to the left of the yellow elevator platform
		-platform will raise bot up to higher level.
		-Before the bot walks off the edge, move the yellow block out of the way.
		-Once bot has walked off, landed, and started moving forward, place block back in it's original spot.
		-Lift up sliding yellow door and allow bot to walk underneith. 
		-Level will restart when bot gets to end
